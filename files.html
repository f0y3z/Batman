Who am I?

Gotham City—Chaos erupted at the Gotham Museum of Antiquities tonight as armed intruders attempted to steal a priceless artifact. Witnesses report a sudden blackout followed by the unmistakable silhouette of the Dark Knight descending from the rafters. Security footage shows Batman taking down the gang with swift precision, disarming explosives planted near the exhibit hall and vanishing into the night before law enforcement arrived. The GCPD confirmed that all suspects have been taken into custody, thanks to the caped vigilante’s intervention.
